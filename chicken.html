Blackened Chicken

A tasty Cajun dinner brimming with seasoning and juicy delight! Perfect for week nights or an easy weekend.

⏱

Prep Time

15 minutes

🍳

Cook Time

20 minutes

🌯

Servings

2 Breasts

The finished chicken, ready to serve.

Ingredients

Prep & Cook

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 1/2 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoon canola oil

All the spices laid out and ready to go.

Instructions

Step 1 — mix the spices

Combine the smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, dried thyme, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, oregano, and salt in a Ziploc bag, and shake to combine.

Step 2 — season the chicken

Pat the chicken breasts dry with paper towels. Brush chicken breasts lightly on both sides with oil or spray with baking spray. Use your hands to spread the seasonings evenly over the chicken.

Make sure to rub into the nooks and crannys.

Step 3 — Prep the pan for the chicken

Heat one tablespoon of canola oil in a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until it is smoking.

Step 4 — Fry the chicken

Cook chicken until it is golden brown (almost black) on both sides and cooked through, approximately 5 minutes on each side.

Spices give the famous recipe its name, "Blackened Chicken".

Step 5 — Rest the chicken

Remove from skillet. Cover with a loose aluminum foil tent; let rest for 10 minutes.

Tips & Variations

Save Some Dishes

Use a Ziploc sandwich bag to mix your spices. You can shake it to combine the spices and throw away the bag when you are done. Happiness equals no extra bowl and spoon to wash.

Butterfly the Chicken

These days chicken breasts are just huge so slice them in half lengthwise. They will still be a healthy portion and they cook more evenly.

Temperature Control

Preheat the pan just until the oil begins to smoke. Add the chicken and sear in those juices and spices. Cook until the chicken is cooked through and browned.
